Luke
Born: Nov 8 2004
Age: 15
Height: 6"3

Power: Metal Skin (Activated by biting his teeth together, downside is that it’s harder to move)

Born in Dublin, Ireland. Later moved to New York with his parents at the age of 6, Luke O’Neill is a lazy and chilled out person. He used to play for a football team for his local school in New York, until he had an injury that made him stay out of a few games. He later decided to apply for All Hero High to try to discover more about his power and use it for more.
